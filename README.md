## About Me

Hello! I'm Mostafa, a self-taught web developer with a unique journey in the technology field. Originally a computer science student, my academic path took an unexpected turn due to political events, leading me to leave college after two years. However, my passion for technology and learning persisted.

### Self-Taught Developer

Embracing the challenge, I delved into web development and embarked on a self-taught journey. Over the course of two years, I acquired proficiency in a variety of technologies, including:

- **HTML:** Building the foundation of web development.

- **CSS:** Styling and enhancing the visual appeal of websites.
- **SCSS (Sass):** Bring a more structured and organized approach to styling, especially in larger projects.
- **JavaScript:** Adding interactivity and dynamic functionality.
- **ReactJS:** Creating responsive and user-friendly front-end interfaces.
- **Git:** Managing version control for collaborative projects.

- **Styled-Components:** Exploring the world of CSS-in-JS, encapsulate styles within components in React applications.

- **Tailwind CSS:** The power of utility-first CSS, Boosting the styling process in my projects.

- **React Router DOM:** Implementing client-side navigation is crucial for creating seamless and dynamic user experiences.

- **React Hook Form:** React Hook Form to simplify form handling, making the development process more straightforward and user-friendly.

### Backend Exploration

Motivated to broaden my skills, I dedicated time to exploring backend technologies. In the last couple of months, I delved into:

- **Node.js:** Enabling server-side JavaScript for scalable applications.
- **Express.js:** Building robust and efficient web applications.
- **MongoDB:** Learning to work with NoSQL databases for data storage.

While I've made strides in backend development, I acknowledge the ongoing learning process and continue to build confidence in this field.
